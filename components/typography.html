Blockquote options

Naming a source

EXAMPLE

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit. Integer posuere erat a ante.

— Someone famous in Source Title

  <footer class="f6 gray">— Someone famous in <cite title="Source Title" class="i">Source Title</cite></footer>
</blockquote>
